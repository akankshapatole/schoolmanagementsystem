Admission Process

Welcome to our admission process. Below are the steps to join our school community:

  1. Complete the online admission form on this page.
  2. Submit necessary documents, including academic transcripts and recommendation letters.
  3. Attend an interview with our admission team.
  4. Receive admission confirmation and further instructions.

If you have any questions or need assistance, feel free to contact us.
